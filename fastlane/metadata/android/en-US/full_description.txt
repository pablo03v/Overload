Overload is a native app with material you design to keep a record of times. Go to work? Track your time! Go to school? Track your time! Do sports? Track your time! All without using the internet or other permissions

Features
- create time spans
- automagically creates pauses in between
- delete time spans - on-by-one or all-together
- scroll through days with ease
